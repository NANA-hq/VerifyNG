VerifyNG is a modern consumer safety and product verification platform designed to help Nigerians identify fake, unsafe, or unregistered products in real time.

Built with accessibility and public safety in mind, VerifyNG combines barcode scanning, ingredient analysis, AI-powered assistance, and counterfeit reporting into one smart platform.

 Why VerifyNG Matters

Counterfeit and unsafe products remain a major challenge across Nigeria, affecting:
- food safety
- medications
- cosmetics
- beverages
- household products

Many consumers cannot easily confirm whether a product is authentic, NAFDAC-approved, or potentially harmful before purchase or use.
VerifyNG was created to make product safety checks faster, easier, and more accessible to everyday users.

What Users Can Do
Users can:

- Scan product barcodes instantly
- Verify NAFDAC registration numbers
- Check product safety information
- Analyze ingredients
- Detect suspicious or counterfeit products
- Receive real-time fake product alerts
- Report counterfeit products
- Access AI-powered safety assistance
- View product trust and safety statistics



 Key Features

Smart Barcode Scanner
Instantly scans products using the device camera for quick verification.

AI Safety Assistant
Interactive assistant that helps users understand ingredients, safety risks, and product information.

Counterfeit Reporting
Users can report fake or suspicious products directly through the platform.

Safety Insights
Provides product verification data and consumer safety statistics.

Fully Responsive Design
Optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

Secure & Accessible
Built with modern web technologies and HTTPS support for secure camera access and safer browsing.
